Significant Song

A song that I really like and that I think has a good meaning is Jesse McCartney's "Beautiful Soul." I like this song not just because is sounds good but the message says that people don't want someone that is only good looking, they want someone who is attractive on the inside too.

Signigicant Piece of Literature

I like Grimm Brother version of Cinderella because it really does show the triumph of good over evil. I also like that the Grimm Brothers added in a religious twist to their version. Even though parts of this story were gruesome it made me realize what some people would do to get what they want. This story really made me see that being humble has huge advantages whether they happen right away or if they take time.

Significant Movie

My significant movie would be A Cinderella Story with Hilary Duff and Chad Michael Murray. I really like this movie of Cinderella because it is based on the present time and has a good message. It is a chick flick with a romantic comedy twist. One thing that I do really like about this movie is it doesn't have any "magic" in it. I think this movie also shows that you should never give up on your dreams no matter how many people want you not to succeed.
