Letter to Me

Dear Zack in the future, How is it going? Are you doing well? If you are, that's good, and if you aren't, that's cool. As long as you're not living with your parents anymore. This is wierd. I have no idea of what I'm doing right now. I hope it's cool. If I'm playing music for a living, then that's cool. If I'm not a musician but I'm like a psychiatrist right now, then that's still awesome( I know I spelled psyichiatrist wrong twice, but whateves). I have a couple of stupid questions because I'll be able to answer them myself, but what is music like in the future? Theres' no way it could've gotten any worse, right? Please tell me I'm right.


Significant Quote(s), Song, Writing, Movie

“Don't cry because it's over, smile because it happened.” Dr. Seuss- This quote means something to me because that's kind of what I do when something that is so fun that I don't want to stop doing it. I don't get really angry or sad when I have to. I just feel happy that I got to do it at all.

The song "Born to Run" by Bruce Springsteen is one of my favorites because I feel this wierd freedom when I hear it. It just talks about what kids were like in the 70's and 80's, and I wish I could've lived back then.

My favorite piece of writing is one that I don't remember the name of. We read it in Amer. Lit. and again it's about teenage angst and running away, and that concept and the era that it takes place in is one of my favorites.

I love the movie Inception because it challenges my mind. I had to watch it three times before I fully understood it, and every time I watch it, I see something that I hadn't seen before.
