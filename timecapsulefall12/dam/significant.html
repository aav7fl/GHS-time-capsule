The Hunger Games

The book, The Hunger Games, is set in the future where people of Panem (North America) are seperated in 12 Districts. Each District must choose one male and one female to fight against each other to the death each year. All of the Hunger Games is televised for everyone to see. In the old and poor District 12, Katniss Everdeen and Peeta Mellark were chosen as tributes to fight. They both tried not to develop a close friendship knowing there can only be one winner. Before the games started, Peeta expressed his true feelings for Katniss saying he has been in love with her. Only to find that Haymitch (their mentor) has a plan to keep them both alive.

The 'nerd' is now popluar and the popular is now the 'geek'. We get to follow the journey of the outrageous things these guys get to do over in highschool.
