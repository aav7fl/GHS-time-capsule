Prediction of Future

Ten years from now, I am hoping that I would be doing something with computers. Whether it's a computer software engineer, or a video game developer, I don't mind what I will be doing as long as it's computer related. I really wanna be a video game developer/programmer and maybe even own my own Indie game company someday. I'm also hoping that I will still be continuing my studies of the Hapkido form of martial arts, and maybe other forms of martial arts. As long as I stick with technology as my field of study in college, I'm sure that I will have a bright future.
