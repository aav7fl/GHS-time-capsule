The game was released on November 8, 2011 The player takes the role of amny different characters during the campaign, its divided into three sets of missions called "Acts". Each mission in an act is a series of objectives. Damage to the player is shown by the percentage of blood splatter shown on the screen. The player's health regenerates over time. Mission objectives vary, ranging from having the player arrive at a particular checkpoint, to eliminating enemies in a specified location, to standing their ground against the enemy, using assult rifles and other weapons, and planting explosives an enemy base or vehicle. Some of these objectives can be failed, and may or may not screw with the story. The player is also accompanied by other soldiers who cannot be told orders.
