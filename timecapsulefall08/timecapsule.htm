Time Capsules

Classes:

  • Fall 2008
  • Winter 2009
  • Fall 2009
  • Winter 2010
  • Fall 2010
  • Winter 2011
  • Fall 2011

Create a web-based "time capsule". It can be just a snapshot of the year, or can encompass everything from K to now. It's up to you
Try to remember and think of things that you did during the year(s).
Give examples of movies, TV, toys, vacations, school activities, etc.
An important component might be a personal writing about your thoughts now on some topic.

"The point, as with all time capsules, is to illustrate life in 2008, 2009, or whatever."

After grading, these time capsules will be sealed and not opened until 10 yrs from now. There will be a link to it, but the link will open to a vault page which will have a countdown timer on it to opening day.
