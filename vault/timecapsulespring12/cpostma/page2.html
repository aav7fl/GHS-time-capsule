My definition of happiness is being able to live your life the way that you want to without anybody telling you what to do.

My definition of freedom is when you are able to say or do anything that you feel to do without being pulled back down by society and conformatism.
A significant quote that I really like is the quote:
"Your mistakes do not define you."
It is a great quote that just means that no matter what you do, how many bad things you do, nobody can make out your personality from those things.
