Movie: The Avengers


The Avengers was released this past weekend, on May 4th, 2012. The long-awaited team-up of Marvel superheroes followed the release of five films featuring each individual Avenger. The movie shattered box-office records, earning the highest grossing domestic opening weekend of all time at $200.3 million. Directed by Joss Whedon, The Avengers highlights the difficulty of six super-powered strong personalities tryign to work together to stop an invasion of the Earth by inter-dimensional beings.

Literature: The Fault In Our Stars


The Fault in Our Stars is the fifth novel by award-winning author and popular Youtube vlogger John Green. The book about a sixteen-year-old cancer patient reached #1 on the New York Times Best Seller list. John Green signed all 150,000 copies of the first edition.
