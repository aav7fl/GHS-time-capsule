Technology in 10 Years


Personal technology will be necessary for all. Government records, ID's, and shopping will move towards integrating people's personal computers as a necessary part of day-to-day life. Public internet access will be available for all across the United States, and in many locations across the globe. Smart phones will be replaced by either glasses or contacts that display information directly to one's eyes, and the interface is easily interacted with by waving hands in the air, using voice commands, or thinking.
