    This are my Predictions. Many things change over ten years and often quite drastically but we can allways try and predict.



    Happiness


    Happiness is a very nebulous term and depends on what a person is looking for in life. Here is my definition now. Maybe it has changed, in the past few years. For me happiness is achieving your goals, and making the best of your life. Trying to all achive what you can and don't get bogged down by other things that you can't achieve.


    Freedom

    For me freedom is having the ability to do what you want. Not being coerced into somethign that you do not want to do. I believe that you should always have a choice in nearly every matter. Don't ever get into a position where you are forced to do as someone else is leading you.


    Prediction For Technology

    Technology ten years from now should be drastically different from what it is now. I predict that the main thing will be miniaturization. Everything will be a lot smaller than what things are now. And of course ten years from now technology will be ten times more powerfull than what we have now.


    Vox Populi, Vox Dei
     Your Past Self Eric.
