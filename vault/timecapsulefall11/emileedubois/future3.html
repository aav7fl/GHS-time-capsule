Media

I love reading, as I'm sure you do, but I never seem to have the time. This year in College Literature, we read A Separate Piece, Lord of the Flies, and A Brave New World. I think I will miss that part of high school. I restarted the Harry Potter books this week. They never get old Also this week I started A Long Way Gone, by Ishmael Baeh. In the Bible, I am reading Romans, and I am really enjoying that. I love my Kindle, but haven't had time to use it! I've read all the Anne of Green Gables Books, and I loved them. Books I want you to go back and read: Lord of the Rings, East of Eden, Pride and Prejudice, Hitchhiker's guide to the Galaxy, Romana, and Nancy Drew.

I have seen so many good movies lately! Super 8, Harry Potter 7B, Tangled, Captain America, Thor, White Christmas, Toy Story 3, A-Team, Baby Momma, Red, Surf Ninjas and Sherlock Holmes. Also, Lord of the Rings, Star Trek, and Star Wars are classics. I also really enjoy Phineas and Ferb, Storage Wars and Psych on T.V. I love watching movies, and I hope you do too.

There are many songs rolling through my head right now. Most of them are Christmas songs because it is December. My favorite versions of classic Christmas songs are those done by Straight No Chasers. I also really enjoy Switchfoot's albums, especially the new one. I really got into country music this year, but don't have any song in particular. The song It Is Well is special in my heart because it reminds me of Grandpa Dubois. Also, I recently discovered the band Gungor, and I really enjoy them. As always, John Williams is always a favorite.
