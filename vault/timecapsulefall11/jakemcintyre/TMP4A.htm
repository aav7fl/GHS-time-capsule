Definition of freedom

My definition of freedom is being able to do what you want without anyone stopping you. Being able to live your life without anyone bossing you around or telling you what to do.

Definition of Happyiness

My definition of happyiness is playing videogames. being able to do what you want, playing sports, and being able to hang out with my friends.
