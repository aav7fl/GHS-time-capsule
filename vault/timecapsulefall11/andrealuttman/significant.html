Significant Piece of Literature

I can think of a few really significant pieces of literature that are really enjoyable to read. One of those is the book The Giver by Lois Lowry. This book really makes you think about all the ever so simple things we take foregranted--color, memories, weather, choices, etc. That makes the book most significant to me. However, there is a corrupt hint behind it. Sometimes I wonder when this type of thing will take place in our world."The Giver is many things to many different people. People bring to it their own complicated sense of beliefs and hopes and dreams and fears and all of that." ~Lois Lowry

Significant Quote
"My horse teaches me everything that I need to know about myself."
Maybe it is not significan to most people, as in most I mean 95%. However, to me and and anyone else who has owned a horse and truly loved horses--it is not a quote. It is the honest to God truth.It is the story of my life and I would not have it any other way. Horses are a mirror for those it has bonded with. It shares your fears, confidence, insecurities, victories, failures, etc. Through it all they are still your best friend. By now you better be passing this quote out through Equine-Assisted Psychotherapy.
