Significant film, Star Trek series collection

The reason that I like Star Trek is because it's aventurous and many times unpredicable unless you've already seen them. During the course of all the series together they go through Enterprise all the way to Enterprise-E. The captains, and the crews, of these starships encounter other races and explore the galaxy. There are two series that don't deal with the Enterprise's those being; Deep Space Nine which deals with a spacestation next to an active worm hole that connects the Alpha Quadrant and the Beta quadrent. The other one being Voyager, the ship get hurled acrossed the galaxy to the other side called the Delta quadrent and the captain sets course back towards home having to deal with numerous problems and unknown races and phenomonons


Star Trek Had inspired me to create Sci-Fi stories of my own, though I try to stay away from using them for a base because their weapons for the Federation Starships are cheesy because in the first series they could already sustain their phasers without burning anything out or losing energy, they are practically unstoppable they are just too stupid to see it. Defences are more realistic so they don't have to worry about that.
