Dear Kathryn,

This is your blast from the past. I assume you probably like to be called Kathryn by now. It's been a while I know. I hope you can still do the Bernie, I'm pretty good at it. I'm a junior at Grandville High School right now. I can't wait for my senior year. I admit I'm nervous. I'm trying really hard to graduate top 50 in my class. I'm 70th something right now. I'm just not sure what I want to do with my life yet. I love hangout with little kids so I hope to work with them, possibly as a teacher. I want to do my basics at GRCC because it's a lot cheaper that way, and then go to a good college that specializes in education, like Grand Valley State University or Central. Let me tell you about myself, or us. I'm a brash, loud, energetic ball of craziness. I'm just stupid sometimes, but you know, I couldn't be happier. I have tons of friends and really good ones too. Rachael is my best friend, and I would love it if we stay friends in the future because shes my second half. Hannah is my other best friend, but she moved to Arizona. However she's coming back senior year and we are going to party everyday. I hope you still want a lot of kids and two dogs like I do now. You are an amazing person Katie Skinner. Lots of love and hope for the future
