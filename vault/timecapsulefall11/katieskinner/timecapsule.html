My Quotes and Definitions

My definition of happiness is being surrounded by people you love, and that love you.

My definition of freedom is the right to act, speak, or think without restrictions or hindrance from others.

I think technology will take a huge leap in future. I doubt Facebook will be around anymore though. Sites like that don't usually stay around for that long. YouTube will probably sky rocket though. I can see it as a major source for media in the future. Apple will probably come out with an Ipod so thin you can fold it up. They will probably have an app that you control a robot to preform real brain surgery or something.

One of my favorite quotes is:

"Do not dwell in the past, do not dream of the future, concentrate the mind on the present moment"- Buddha.
