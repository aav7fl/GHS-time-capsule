These are my thoughts about the future of technology...
Ten years from now, I except technology not to be too far advanced from where it is now. In our semi recessionary economy at the moment, there is less innovation and we don't' have time to think freely of new possibilities and ideas. We are also a breaking point with being fed up with the rich. Too many things are happening in the world to worry about advancing tech. I do suppose that SSD drive will be the norm for higher end computers, and hard drive disks will start to phase out to the lower mid laptops. I honestly don't expect to see too much happen. I feel like we are an inverse derivative tangent approaching an asymptote. Our Advance in technology is the Y value, while our time is X. As we continue our years, we are gaining less in the tech field due to actual physical limits of silicon and computer chips.
