Happiness:


My definiton of happiness is excitement, satisfaction, thrilled, having postive emotions, joy, bliss. Happiness means having a good life; married with kids, or finding that first job, or maybe receiving a big reward. Happiness, for me, is all those things put together. It allows us to enjoy moments of life; video games, sports, summer. Without happiness, there would be bad, negative emotions, and excitement would turn calm, indifferent.




Freedom:


Freedom is never having to take orders from anyone, never having to be a slave again. Going off on your own, being able to fend for yourself, finally discovering who your truly are. It's the power of control over decisions without any restraint.
