My definition of Happiness...


Happiness to me is not having to worry about anything in life. Being able to relax and not think about anything. Having a good time with your friends and family. Having someone else care about you. Caring about someone else. Doing what you want. Everything being simple and easy. Happiness can mean a lot of things but that's what it means to me.

My definition of Freedom...


Freedom to me is doing whatever you want to when you want and how you want to. Freedom is not having someone always telling you what to do and being able to express yourself freely. Nobody judging you for doing something or wearing something. Freedom can have a lot of meanings but that is what it means to me.
