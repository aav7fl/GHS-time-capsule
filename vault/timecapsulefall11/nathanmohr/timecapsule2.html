A letter to you

Nathan 10 years ago you were planning on attending GRCC. After that you were going to attend GVSU. You were obsessively interested in videogames. Your Grandmother had just died and you received here bed and dresser to upgrade your room. You dreaded finishing your 12th grade year and really wanted to be done with high school. Every morning you would go to the library to play YUGIOH with your friends. While you were in high school you were a slacker I hope that, that changed when you attended college. Did you accomplish anything with your life? You were hoping to join a government law enforcement agency. You loved order and rules always adhering to them or creating rules where none existed. So what did you do with your life? Is it good what do you regret about your decisions?

Definitions of life

Happiness is being satisfied with your station in life. You enjoy what you do and live life to the fullest. Happiness is when you can live without any regrets and without any remorse for lost opportunities.

Freedom is the ability to do what you want when you want to do it within the bounds of the law and institutions that govern the country. Freedom is also the ability to attepmt to enact change on laws that are perceived as being unfair or unjust.
