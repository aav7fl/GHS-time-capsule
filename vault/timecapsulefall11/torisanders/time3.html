Pop culture items from 2011...


The Twilight saga has become very popular and this year Breaking Dawn came out in theaters. The books and especially the movies have become a growing phenominon.
The Harry Potter series has been growing since 2000. The last and final movie The Deathly Hallows: Part 2 came out in July. Harry Potter has always a popular topic throughout the years.

Jersey Shore is a reality TV show on MTV. It features 8 friends who live together in New Jersey at a party house, and they go clubbing every night and "GTL" (Gym, Tan, Laundry) daily. The third season of Jersey Shore this year took place in Italy, where they trashed their apartment and disgraced the American race.
Teen Mom/16 and Pregnant is also a reality TV show on MTV. The show first started as 16 and Pregnant, documenting the lives of teenage girls who got pregnant during high school. The show grew into Teen Mom, which follows only a few of the girls from 16 and Pregnant, and shows how their lives have changed being a young mother and having a child.

Justin Beiber is a new teenage heart-throb pop artist. His popular singles such as "Baby", "One Time", and "Somebody to Love" have stolen the hearts of teenage girls around the world. Justin was recently accused of having fathered a child to one of his fans, which was proven false after he took a paternity test.
Katy Perry is a popular pop artist. She came out with her first hit single "I Kissed a Girl" in 2008, which led up to the release of her album "Teenage Dream" in 2010 which features other popular songs including "Teenage Dream", "The One That Got Away", and "Last Friday Night T.G.I.F.".
