Class Description

1st Hour

Advanced 3D CAD

Mr. Denning

In Adv. 3D CAD, we make assembly drawings of machinery and tools using a computer program to help generate the images we draw.

2nd Hour

Honors Physics

Mr. Evele

In Hon. Physics we learn about physics at a faster pace than the rest of the classes and we learn about things such as: Momentum, Gravity, Forces, etc.

3rd Hour

College Lit.

Ms. Gergen

In College Lit. we read books that are on the college level and after we have read through the book we analyze many aspects of the book such as theme or hidden meanings.

4th Hour (B Lunch)

Pre-Calc

Mrs. Hildenbrand

In Pre-Calc we are taught about many trigonometry aspects and about Sine and Cosine waves.

5th Hour

Intro to Web Design

Mr. Dolloff

In Intro to Web Design we learn the language of HTML and learn how to build websites of our very own using HTML Kit or Angelfire.

6th Hour

Wood Tech

Mr. Herbert

In Wood Tech we build furniture out of wood and in the process of doing this we learn about the different species of wood and the different tools in wood working.
