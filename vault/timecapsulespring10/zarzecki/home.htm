*Who Am I?*

My name is Steven Zarzecki. My friends call me Scuba. I am 18 years old and attending Grandville Highschool for my Senior year. I plan to go to Grand Valley State University after graduation to study to become a mechanical engineer then go on to Motorcycle Mechanics Institute of Technology to get my ceritfication in motorcycle repair. With my knowledge in engineering and motorcycle mechanics I hope to get picked up by the Honda Motocross team working in their R and D department or working as a race mechanic with their racers.


*Free Time*

In my free time I work on and ride my motorcycles. I ride a modified Honda CR125R and for fun I ride my Kawasaki KLX110 pitbike which is also modified and souped up to look and ride like my 125 race bike. In the summer I ride Motocross at tracks such as Big Air in Newaygo, MX131 in Martin, Sandbox MX in Vassar, and Baja MX in Birch-Run. I like to ride Motocross because its a good way for me to get out stress and I love the Adrenaline Rush. I also like to play my guitar too, but if the weather is nice I'm out ridin'.


*Favorite Food*

My favorite kind of food is Pasta. I love Alfredo especially when there is chicken and brocaoli in it. I dont like fast food or many fried things. I usually keep my diet pretty normal and healthy so I have enough energy to ride at the level I have to. Fast food makes me laggy and tired.
