Autumn, Fall. The beginning of the end. Usually associated with leaves falling from trees and the end of the sunshine. Well, at least it is if you live in England where the weather is notoriously bad. Anyway, here is where your description will go.

Introduction.

So, here's my entry to the autumn competition. Originally I planned to use some form of stock photography for the header image but I thought that most likely everybody would do that. So I decided to draw my own. This has created the theme for the whole template. The main things that remind me of autumn are obviously the falling leaves, warm colours and cold skies. These things I have tried to implement in this design. Next to this paragraph is an image so you can see how they are formatted.
So to clear that up:

  • Falling Leaves
  • Warm Colours
  • And Cold Skies.
