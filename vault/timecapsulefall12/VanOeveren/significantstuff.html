Significant Stuff

Significant Quote:

"Do, or do not. There is no try." - Yoda (Star Wars Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back). I am a huge Star Wars fan and I really love this quote. It really inspires me to try new things which is why I like it.

Significant Song/Lyrics:

"You Raise Me Up" by Josh Groban. This song is significant to me because it was my Grandma's favorite song and it even played at her funeral. Whenever I listen to it it reminds me of her and all the good times we had together.

Significant Piece of Literature:

The Percy Jackson and the Olympians series by Rick Riordan is one of my favorite book series. It was one of the only series of books that I got into and it also got me interested in Greek Mythology. Rick Riordan remains my favorite author to this day.

Significant Movie:

The Avengers is probably one of my most favorite movies of all time. I am a huge fan of Marvel super hero movies and in my opinion this one was the best one. There was a lot of humor in it and a good amount of action as well. I am really excited for The Avengers 2 which will come out in 2015.
