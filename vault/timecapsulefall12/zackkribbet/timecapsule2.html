Happiness/Freedom in My Eyes

Happiness to me is kind of weird. Some people think that they can only be happy if they're with their friends or doing something, which is true sometimes for me. But I always feel happy just laying back and watching T.V. or playing my guitar. I know that makes me seem like a loner, but I don't mind. Happiness should be when you look around and think, "This is good. I like it here." And freedom for me is probably the same for a lot of kids. All I need to have freedom is the ability to do something that I want to do when I want to do it. On the trip to Chicago with the German and French classes, we got to walk around the city by ourselves. That is freedom to me.


Significant Quote(s), Song, Writing, Movie

"All education is is a series of deteriorating lies."

-Mr. Evele-Yea, thats' right. Mr. Evele said this quote, and I thought it was hilarious, but I also thought it was true. When we're young, I think we are tought these lies because some of the stuff we learn in the future is pretty harsh, so they are just trying to preserve our innocence.

I love the song "Sympathy for the Devil" by the Stones because it is full of mystery. Listen to it and try to hear all of the lyrics, and it's cool. Plus-and this is kind of weird-the number 666 shows up in my life much more than it should. At a cross country meet, my number was 666. Thats' weird.

I read "The Da Vinci Code" a few years ago, and I stll remember how awesome it was. It seriously was an amazing book. Suspense waited for you on every page. Each sentence made you think, "Oh my god, what's gonna happen next?" That's a good book.

"Silence of the Lambs" is another one of my favorite movies because it combines action and crime fighting with sociopaths and all kinds of stuff. Most people think that I'm creepy for liking that movie, and they're probably right. It's messed up.
