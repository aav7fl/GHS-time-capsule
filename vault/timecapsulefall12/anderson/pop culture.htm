Popular movies of 2012

The Hunger Games

The Hunger Games was one of the most highly anticipated movies of the year. Based off the trilogy by Suzanne Collins.

The Dark Knight Rises

The last installment of a trilogy. Starting with Batman Begins, The Dark Knight, and now The Dark Knight Rises.

The Avengers

One of the biggest movies to hit the big screen ever. It set records the first weekend!

The Hobbit

The highly anticipated prequel to the Lord of the Rings triology finally came out this year. It is the first of 3 parts.
