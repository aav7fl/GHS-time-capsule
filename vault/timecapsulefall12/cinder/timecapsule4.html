I think that within the next ten years many things in this world will change. I believe that people will become more dependent on technology and other people to do their work. I also think that war will become more common. Maybe not in the traditional hand-to-hand combat but more by chemical and biological warfare. I wouldn't be surprised if the government continued to become stronger and control the lives of the American people more. 2012 and 2013 are crucial years to find out where the world will be in the next ten. Only time will tell where the United States decides to go from here.
