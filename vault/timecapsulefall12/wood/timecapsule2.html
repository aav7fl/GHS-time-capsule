My definition of happiness: The point of being completely relaxed, not worried about tomorrow, about the future. Being able to say you're finally just all around satisfied with yourself, and the people around you. When you can walk the world without a care of what someone thinks.

My definition of freedom: Being able to think, feel, and do what you want, while being treated equally.
