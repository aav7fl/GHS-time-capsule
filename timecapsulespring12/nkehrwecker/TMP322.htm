Letter to me

Your definition of happiness should be enjoying your life, not doing drugs or any other stupid stuff. And spending a lot of time with family, and your girlfriend if you haven't married her yet.

Freedom

Your freedom is living your life how you want, not how others want you too. Doing what you want, and what you love.

Technology
