Sean gave me this phone because he was no longer using Verizon's service and he just loves me so much! ;) It's only May & I've already had it replaced 3 times...lets just say I'm not to great with my phones...first time I was angry and threw it at the wall, not smart! Next thing, it was just dumb and I had to talk to people through speaker phone. & this last time was because it kept turning off, that was the most annoying thing about this phone. The battery had a 'so called' bad connection with the phone so merely bumping it could turn it off. So far so good though with this new one! Lets hope it last's till this December so I can get a sweet new one. Other then having it replaced so much I really like this phone, it's fairly simple to use and pretty small!
