My Definition Happiness and Freedom



Define Happiness
Happiness to me is being independent. Happiness is loving who you are so much that you won't change for anyone, no matter how much they may not like it. Its living life for you and doing what makes you happy because at the end of the day there will always be someone who doesn't approve.


Define Freedom
Freedom to me is being able to be independent (take care of yourself), living on my own. Freedom is not having your parents to "baby-sit" me, being able to go out when I want, without having to ask for permission. I'm even willing to say that being free can make me happy
