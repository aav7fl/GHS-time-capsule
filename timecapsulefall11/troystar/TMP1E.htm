A Letter To Myself

Hey Future Troy,

This is you from the year 2011. I'm guessing the year now is about 2021, and you're about 26. I'm betting everything way different than is is now in the year 2011. So tell me, is the music any better in 2021? I'm guessing it ain't much better than it is here. You remember when we listened to Frank Sinatra, Dean Martin and the rest of the Rat Pack? Now, Ain't That A Kick In The Head? Remeber, good music never dies.

So, is there a bright future ahead for us? And no I don't mean a atom bomb. What do you do for a living? Are you a doctor, or history teacher? Are you married and have kids? If you are, then good luck to you. Just remember, kids never go away. And if you don't have kids, I'd say that's good, who wants to be married and taking care of kids at 26? Not me.

So what's the style that kids have in your time? I'm sure it's different than what it is now. But who cares? It's all stupid anyway.

And finally, always rememeber that things ain't as bad as they seem, something good always comes out of a bad situation, take every day as it comes, and the best revenge is massive success.

This is you from the year 2011, telling you to always remember to live till you die.

-Troy Star 2011

About The Above Quote

A man named Frank Sinatra once said "I'm gonna live till I die." and that's exactly what he did. This is my favorite quote because it says you should enjoy everything life has to offer and to keep living it up and enjoying life until you're dead.
