name significant piece of literature

     I would have to say that my significant piece of literature would be Eragon, it's such a good book that at times I found if hard to put it down if memory serves me correctly. It was realistic on how the author did it. Eragon being a farmer in the countryside going about his own business on a hunting expedition to hunt game, deer. Instead of bringing a slayed deer back he brings back an egg which he thinks is a stone at first. Events started to unfold based on that one action. That was the first book of a series of four or five books
