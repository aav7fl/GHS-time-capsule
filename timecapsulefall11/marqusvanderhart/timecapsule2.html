A Letter From the Past
Happiness- A lot of people think happiness is having fame, fortune, and being popular or famous... but I think happiness is more like enjoying your life and just loving your friends and family and always thinking about god. That just my deffinition.

Hello future Marqus, it's kind of weird writing this letter to you

and dont know exactly what to say. I feel like your living a very

successful life and living the single life and hopefully no kids yet.

I just hope you havent made a really dumb mistakes like you did

as a kid, because mistakes as an adult are more serious. As I'm typing

this I feel like I know alot but I bet you know alot more as a

twenty-seven year old. I know you didn't do your best as a high school

student but I know you're more educated about stuff that matters in life

like God, family, friends, and your career Just remember that as long as

you pray and keep faith in god, everything will be alright. I hope you

changed a lot from what I am because I'm just like my father and you don't

know how much I've despised that all my life.

Sincerely, the past YOU, Marqus Vander Hart
