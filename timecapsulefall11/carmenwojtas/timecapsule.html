Hi Carmen! :)

Dear future me,

Hey there, I know it's been a while since you were my age. But I want to remind you about a few things you knew when you were seventeen.

First off, remember to love with all you heart. This is one of the most important things in life. It may hurt to get your heart broken, but there's no reward without risk.

Remember to give others the grace that God himself gives you. Even though you morally strive for justice, don't judge people, that's up to God, so give the gavel back to him.

Remember to keep your priorities straight. Do what needs to be done, but remember to give yourself a break. Life's not a race; it's a journey, so enjoy it. Things may get hard, or even be hard for you right now, but keep your head up. I've seen dark times, and I know the best way to get through them is by letting God guide you and by staying positive. Joy is something that you can always have, even when happiness seems impossible.

And finally, remember not to take yourself too seriously. Laugh, joke, goof around. Have fun! Don't become an over-stoic SADuldt. Don't lose your inner child :).
