A few definitions of things and how i would define them right now.

My definition of freedom is well... there are too many kinds of freedom. there could be freedom from government and all that. which that is being able to practice your own religion whenever and whatever kind you want in a private area. but then there could also be freedom from pain or from anything else like that. freedom of just doing what you want when you want. not having a relationship just doing whatever you want for your own happiness and no one elses.
