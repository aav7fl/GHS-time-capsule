A significant movie from 2011...

Well, the Harry Potter series ended this year. Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 came out in July, 2011. It was heartbreaking that the series ended, but the movie was so incredibly good! You saw it twice, and it should be coming out on DVD/BluRay soon.. I hope you're not laughing at "DVDs" yet... But this movie is significant, for one, like I said, it ended the 10 year Harry Potter movie series... Which was a huge chunk of your childhood. I wonder if Daniel Radcliffe and all of the cast ever starred in any other movies? Guess I'll know when you read this. But anyways, I would consider this a very significant movie from 2011, along with some other of my favorites such as The Hangover Part II, No Strings Attached, Just Go With It, Your Highness, and Bridesmaids. I hope you remember how much of a movie buff you were! ;)
