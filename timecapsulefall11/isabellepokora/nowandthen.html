Prediction of Technology in the Future

Technology changes so fast... I remember dad once saying that he got sick of buying the newest computers and what not because it would just be obsolete within a few weeks... This really hasn't changed. Just look at how much cell phones have changed in the past 10 years. It's crazy!

I had a few predictions for technology. I'm thinking that virtual gaming will be the norm in ten years. I hope that we will be able to really interact with our videogames. First Person Shooters were really popular back in the day. Could imagine having a virtual experience of that?

I also hope that we have holographic watches... Or something awesome like that. Or hopefully my car can fold up into a suitcase. I dunno, but I'm sure technology will surprise me.
